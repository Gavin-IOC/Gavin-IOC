Problems Customers Face

  • Interest, but with difficult accessibility
  • Lack of autonomy when traveling in China
  • Inconvenience in Chinese services
  • Language barrier in every aspect
  • Lack-of-trust in Chinese services
  • Inconvenience in visa and documentation

Our Solution

  • ONE clear,O2O, all-inclusive site that caters to every need
  • Assistive timeline sequencing from most to least essential service
  • Consumers receive partial to full autonomy based on selection of services
  • Consumer based rating and feedback system for quality assurance
  • Offline traditional services mixed with online technology that eliminates language barrier
